Patatas Bravas Recipe

Description

This a common tapa in spain, but it has many variants. This one is very typical in Valencia, just fried potatoes with Allioli and a slight touch of paprika.

It is also a very simple recipe, but hard to master.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes - 500 g
  • 1 spoonful of paprika
  • Olive oil for frying - Around 200 ml (as needed)
  • Allioli - around 200 g
  • Salt - as desired

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and clean them.
  2. Cut them in small dices, around 3 cm long.
  3. Heat a pan with olive oil at around 90 degrees C.
  4. Cook the potatoes until they are ready. We need them soft, but not crispy.
  5. To make them a bit crispy, put more heat when they are already cooked. Be careful not to burn them.
  6. Put them into a plate, with napkins at the bottom to absorb the excess oil.
  7. Add some salt as desired.
  8. Pour the allioli on top of them or at the side.
  9. Sprinkle the paprika around the potatoes and more importantly on the allioli.
